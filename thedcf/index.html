Intake — work requests

Anyone on the team submits requests here; each one enters the approvals queue (Manager review, then Director sign-off) and appears in the Intake column of the workflow board.

Approvals queue

Two-step routing: a Manager reviews first, then a Director (or the CEO) gives final sign-off. Add a note with either decision and it is sent to the employee who made the request. Buttons enable only for the role whose turn it is — switch "Viewing as" in the header to act each step.

Notes are delivered to the requesting employee and, like every decision, are time-stamped against the acting role in the audit log — dispute-grade traceability.
